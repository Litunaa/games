FAQ

1. How do I place an order?

Either press the "purchase" button under the desired game on the Home page, or click on one of the game's picture to get to the game-specific site and then press the "Add To Cart" button.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

Paypal, Debit, Credit

3. Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

No.

4. Can I modify or cancel my order after it's been placed?

No

5. What platform(s) are your digital games compatible with?

It differs from game to game, but mainly PC. Some games are also compatible with console.

6. How can I contact your customer support team?

You can't

7. How do I create an account on your website?

Press the "Sign up" link at the top of the home page
